Data Design¶

Data science would hardly be a science without data. It is thus of utmost importance that we begin any data analysis by understanding how our data were collected.

In this chapter, we will discuss data design, the process that we use to collect our data. Many have drawn premature conclusions because they were not careful enough in understanding their data design; we will use examples and simulations to justify the importance of probability sampling in conducting data science.
